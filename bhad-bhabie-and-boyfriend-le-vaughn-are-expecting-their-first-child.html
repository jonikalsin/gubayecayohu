BHAD BHABIE AND BOYFRIEND LE VAUGHN ARE EXPECTING THEIR FIRST CHILD

Bhad Bhabie, real name Danielle Bregoli, is expecting her first child with boyfriend Le Vaughn! The 20-year-old rapper, known for her infamous catchphrase “Cash me outside, how ’bout that?” during a 2016 appearance on “Dr. Phil,” took to Instagram on Friday to share the joyous news with her followers.

Sharing a couple photos on her Instagram, Bhabie showed off her growin baby bump in a white T-shirt.

The announcement triggered a mix of reactions from fans, with some playfully expressing disbelief, “Please don’t tell me you’re pregnant,” and others recalling earlier predictions, “Hahahaha who else guessed this chick would be pregnant early on in her life.”

Bhabie recently made a comeback on Instagram on November 11th to wish her boyfriend a happy birthday. Sharing a photo of the couple sitting courtside at an NBA game,she captioned, “Happy Birthday baby” accompanied by heart emoji.

Bhad Bhabie rose to fame after her viral appearance on “Dr. Phil,” and her life in the public eye was documented in the 2019 Snapchat series “Bringing Up Bhabie.” Reflecting on her rapid rise to fame, she remarked in the show, “I’m the bad kid that my mother can’t control, so she put me on a TV show. I went from sleeping on the floor in a trailer to having a gold record in six months.”

The rapper took a hiatus from social media in 2020, citing concerns about her mental health. In a candid post, she expressed the impact of social media on her life, stating, “Social media has not only made me who I am but also is every reason I can’t sleep at night.”

While this will be the first child for Bhad, Le Vaughn is already a father to a son.
