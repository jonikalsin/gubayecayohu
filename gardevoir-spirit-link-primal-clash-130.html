Gardevoir Spirit Link (Japanese: サーナイトソウルリンク Sirnight Soul Link) is a Pokémon Tool card. It was first released as part of the Primal Clash expansion.

Card text

Attach a Pokémon Tool to 1 of your Pokémon that doesn't already have a Pokémon Tool attached to it.
Your turn does not end if the Pokémon this card is attached to becomes M Gardevoir-EX.
You may play as many Item cards as you like during your turn (before your attack).

Release information

This card was included in the Primal Clash expansion, first released in the Japanese Tidal Storm expansion. It was reprinted in the Steam Siege expansion, first released in the Japanese Cruel Traitor expansion. In Japan, it was reprinted in The Best of XY subset and the Extra Regulation Box.
