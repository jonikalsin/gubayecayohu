New Update: 15-Year-Old Ridge Girl Who Went Missing Twice In Week Found

A Long Island teenager who was reported missing twice in a week has been found.

Shyma Dahoui, age 15, left a relative’s home in Shoreham, located on Vee Jay Drive, at around 1:30 a.m. Tuesday, May 30, according to Suffolk County Police.

On Sunday, June 4, police announced that Dahoui had been located and was unharmed.

Original story:

Police are asking for help in locating a Long Island teenager who has gone missing for the second time in a week.

Shyma Dahoui, age 15, left a relative’s home in Shoreham, located on Vee Jay Drive, at around 1:30 a.m. Tuesday, May 30, according to Suffolk County Police.

It came just days after the teen was found unharmed after previously being reported missing from another home in the hamlet of Ridge on Wednesday, May 24.

Dahoui is described as a Black female, approximately 5-feet tall and 115 pounds, with black hair and brown eyes.

She was last seen wearing gray sweatpants, a black hooded sweatshirt, and pink flip flops.

Anyone with information on her whereabouts is asked to contact the Suffolk County Police Department at 631-852-8752 or call 911.
