Amita Suman Bio (Wiki)

Who is Amita Suman?

Amita Suman is a Nepalese-born English actress. The public noticed her for appearing in known for The Outpost and Shadow and Bone.

How many siblings does Amita Suman have?

She was born on 19 July 1997 in the Terai region, Nepal. Hence, her mother tongue is Bhojpuri.

She spent her childhood in village areas of the landlocked country. The name of her mother and her father is unknown. Her grandparents live in Nepal.

Talking about her siblings, she has a sister Mandip Gill who is also an actress.

She and her family migrated to Brighton, UK when she was seven years of age. She said that she learned to read, speak, and write in English after moving to the UK.

Amita’s ancestry is Nepalese and she had dual(Nepalese-British) citizenship.

Talking about her academics, she attended Sussex Downs College and the Academy of Live and Recorded Arts. From the latter, she passed out in the year 2018.

Amita Suman- Professional Life, Career, Awards

Amita Suman started her acting career by appearing as Nina Biswas in Casualty TV Series for the two episodes in 2018.

In the same year, appeared in an episode of Doctor Who as Umbreen and Ackley Bridge as Sameera. She also did a short screen project named Daughter (2019) as Lilah.

In addition, for her character of Naya in the fantasy series The Outpost S2, she received much appreciation. Her NetFlix series, Shadow and Bone is also on the news for her amazing acting skill.

In this show, she is playing the character of Inej Ghafa and it was aired in April 2021. The other main casts are Jessie Mei Li, Ben Barnes, Archie Renaux, etc.
