Claire Stone Obituary, Augusta GA, What Happened To Claire Stone?

Claire Stone Obituary, Death Cause – In loving memory of Claire Lee Stone, a remarkable soul who graced this world with her presence for 72 years. Claire was born on March 20, 1951, in North Carolina and departed from our lives on October 25, 2023. Her passing leaves behind a void that can never be filled, but her memory will forever be a source of inspiration and love. Claire’s life was a testament to the values of love, compassion, and selflessness. Her journey was marked by her dedication to her family, her career, and her unwavering commitment to making a difference in the lives of those around her.

A Career at Meybohm Real Estate

Claire Stone was a devoted professional who lent her skills and expertise to the real estate industry. She found her niche at Meybohm Real Estate, where she worked diligently and made a mark in the field. Her passion for real estate shone through in her commitment to helping clients find their dream homes and her dedication to the art of negotiation. Her colleagues and clients recognized her for her unwavering integrity, her genuine compassion, and her tenacity in ensuring that her clients’ needs were met. Claire’s professionalism was a reflection of her character, a character that exuded warmth and care.

A Private Memorial Service

In honor of Claire Lee Stone, a private memorial service will be held in the coming months. The family is in the process of finalizing the details of this service and will provide further information to those who wish to pay their respects and celebrate Claire’s life. In Claire’s memory, the family kindly requests that donations be made to the Neurology Stroke Program Fund at Augusta University. This fund is a testament to Claire’s compassionate nature and her commitment to making a positive impact on the world.
