Sanjay Kapoor Age, Biography, Wife, Family, Children &amp; More

Sanjay Kapoor  is an Indian film actor and producer born on 17 October 1965. He is known for  his hit Raja form movie raja and Anant Mathur on Dil Sambhal Jaa Zara that aired on Star Plus.

He is the founder and director of Sanjay Kapoor Entertainment Private Limited company with his wife Maheep Sandhu. The debut movie produced by their production house was Tevar.

He made his  first debut in Hindi cinema in 1995 with the movie Prem.Although both debutants rendered fairly decent performances, the movie was delayed for many years as it was in production since 1989. On release, it bombed at the box office. Kapoor’s next movie was Raja (1995) that is the most hit movie of sanjay kapoor opposite Madhuri Dixit which was a box office success. He went on to star in several films as a lead actor such as Auzaar (1997), Mohabbat (1997) and Sirf Tum (1999). He didn’t have much success as a lead actor as most of his films failed at the box office. In 2002, he appeared as a villain playing the psychotic husband of Esha Deol in Koi Mere Dil Se Poochhe. His performance was praised by critics.

He made his debut on television in 2003 appearing in the serial Karishma – The Miracles of Destiny opposite Karisma Kapoor. He played supporting roles in films like Qayamat: City Under Threat (2003), Julie (2004), Luck By Chance (2009) and Shaandaar (2015).
