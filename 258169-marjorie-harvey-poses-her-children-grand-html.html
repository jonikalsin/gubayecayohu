Marjorie Harvey Poses with Her Children &amp; Grandkids Surrounded by Lavish Easter Decor (Photos)

Marjorie Harvey, the wife of doting grandfather Steve Harvey, appears to love spending the holidays with her grandchildren. Recently, she took to Instagram expressing just how much she enjoys family time.

Marjorie Harvey posed with her kids and grandchildren among lavish Easter decorations in a family picture she shared on Instagram. She also shared some other festive moments via a story she posted on the platform.

The snapshot was incredibly heartwarming, with nearly the entire family joining in the photo and an Easter bunny joining in on the celebrations. The story generally documented the good times the kids were having on the beautiful holiday.

Marjorie and Steve Harvey at the 2014 Steve & Marjorie Harvey Foundation Gala on May 3, 2014 in Chicago, Illinois. | Photo: Getty Images

HAPPY EASTER

Marjorie sat front and center of the picture, holding what appeared to be a bunny rabbit and smiling joyfully at the camera. Taking to the post, she decided to celebrate the festive holiday, writing:

"Happy Easter."

Two of the little ones also gently held fluffy bunny rabbits while the huge dressed-up and white bunny sat on the side. The whole family sat on steps, with colorful balloons in the background, adding to the festive feeling.

THE SPIRIT OF EASTER

In the Instagram story, the children were enjoying themselves profusely. They were either playing with the large white mascot-looking bunny, the real rabbits, presents, balloons, or each other, all in the spirit of Easter.

Morgan, Jason, and Lori were actually conceived by Marjorie and her ex-husband...but Steve has adopted them.

In the last part of the story, two of the grandchildren were cutely and comfortable sitting on a chair together. The one had a little rabbit in his hand, looking at it with adoration, while the other boy pointed at it, a comical look on his face.

Two of Marjorie and Steve Harvey’s grandchildren playing with a rabbit while sitting on a chair. | Source: Instagram/marjorie_harvey

DOTING GRANDDAD

Although Steve is not featured in any of these Easter-focused Instagram pictures or stories, he certainly is a doting grandfather. Speaking about his family and specifically his seven grandchildren, Steve expressed:

"It's really absolutely wonderful, you know? I hug 'em and then I walk off. That's it. You hungry? Go get your mama."

Continuing, he said that his grandchildren love him because he feeds them whatever they want. In terms of his whole family life in general, the television presenter said he likes to keep it private and down low.

Marjorie and Steve Harvey’s grandchildren playing with a dressed up “rabbit” on Easter. | Source: Instagram/marjorie_harvey

FAMILY LOVE

At the end of last year, Steve expressed his adoration for his grandchildren by posting an adorable picture of himself with all of them. The photo was sweet as Steve held the youngest one in his arm, surrounded by all the others.

The comment section went absolutely insane, remarking on how loving the wholesome family looked and the grandchildren's cuteness. So far, the post has received just over 120,000 likes in total.

Who exactly conceived this sweet bunch of grandchildren? Of course, they all belong to the television presenters' seven children: Karli, Brandi, Broderick, Wynton, Morgan, Jason, and Lori.

Morgan, Jason, and Lori were actually conceived by Marjorie and her ex-husband Darnell Woods but Steve has adopted them. All of the other children were conceived by Steve and his two ex-wives.
