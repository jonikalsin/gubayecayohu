Kristen Ledlow - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, In Relation, Nationality

Kristen Ledlow is an American sports anchor. Kristen Ledlow currently serves on NBA TV as the host of  “NBA Inside Stuff”.  Furthermore, she worked as a sportscaster for HLN and CNN prior to her current career at “Good News Show”.

Early life and Childhood

Born on 18th January 1988,  Kristen Ledlow’s present age is 33 years old. Similarly, her father is Roger Ledlow and her mother is Rita Ledlow. She grew up with a sister named Lauren Ledlow and she is originally from Atlanta, Georgia, .

She was interested in the world of sports and hence she played basketball and volleyball in school. Ledlow’s nationality is American. Furthermore, there is no detailed information regarding her ethnicity.

Talking about her educational background, she attended Christian Community School. Later, she also attended North Florida Christian School. She was enrolled at Southeastern University in Lakeland. Furthermore, she majored in broadcasting and communications and minored in business.

Professional life and Career

Talking about Ledlow’s professional life, she initially was an American volleyball player in her school team. In addition, she also served as lead announcer for the men’s basketball games in numerous tournaments. In her senior year, she won the titles of “Miss Capital City USA”. Since graduating from college, Kristen became a reporter on WTXL-TV. Eventually, she hosted the ‘Good News Show’.

Currently, she works for NBA TV as the host of NBA Inside Stuff. And, she is also a courtside reporter for the NBA on TNT.
