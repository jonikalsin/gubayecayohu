Lamprey Was A Delicacy Among Medieval British Elites, Discovery Proves

published on July 02, 2024
Despite its nightmarish appearance, lamprey was a favorite dish among the British elite in medieval times.Wikimedia CommonsThe mouth of a sea lamprey. Despite its vampire-like characteristics and a mouth that looks like something straight out of a horror movie, the lamprey was a popular delicacy amongst British aristocrats in the medieval era. And now, archaeologists in London have discovered physical proof of this scary-looking snack for the first time. During excavations of a cesspit near the Mansion House train station in London, a team of archaeologists came across a few extremely rare lamprey teeth, according to a statement from the Museum of London Archaeology (MOLA).
