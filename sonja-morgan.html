Professional Life and Career

Sonja started her career as a luxury brand consultant for the country’s top restaurants and fashion establishments. She established her fashion brand named Sonja Morgan Brands and debuted her collection in the 2015 New York Fashion Week. Because of her affordable price, her luxury fashion wear and collections were in high demand.

After her great success in the fashion world, she stepped into film production and produced the film “The Marsh” in 2006.

Looking at her success as a fashion entrepreneur, she was invited and cast in the 3rd season of the reality TV show “The Real Housewives of New York City”. She made a total of 185 appearances during the series’ run from 2010-2019.

In 2019, she also appeared in her self-produced drama “The Man in the Attic”.

She is also a donor or contributor to the welfare of children, women, animals, and the LGBTQ community. She has helped such foundations as New Yorker for Children, The Princess Grace Foundation, Jeffrey Fashion Cares, ASPCA, and GLAAD. Her charitable works have gained her numerous titles, including Broadway, and Speaks Out Hero Award.
