Extremely Rare White Giraffe Caught On Video

published on May 28, 2024
The mother and her calf suffer from a genetic condition called leucism. Park rangers at the Ishaqbini Hirola Conservancy in eastern Kenya happened upon two extremely rare white giraffes, and caught the encounter on video. The footage features two white reticulated giraffes, a mother and her calf walking together through the trees. The giraffes appear to have the pigmentation-inhibiting genetic condition of leucism.
