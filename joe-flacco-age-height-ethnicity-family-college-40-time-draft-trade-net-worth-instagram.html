Age and birthday

Joe Flacco was born on January 16, 1985, in Audubon, New Jersey, United States. Joe is currently 37 years old.

Nationality and ethnicity

Height and weight

Joe Flacco stands at a height of 6 feet and 5 inches, which when converted into meters, will be 1.98, or 198 centimeters, and he weighs 111 kg, which is equivalent to 245 lbs.

College

He attended and played collegiate football at the University of Delaware after transferring from the University of Pittsburgh.

Joe was drafted by the Baltimore Ravens in the first round of the 2008 NFL Draft.
