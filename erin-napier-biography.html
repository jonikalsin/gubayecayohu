Who is Erin Napier?

Erin Napier is an American TV reality star, actress, and Host.

Erin is famous for Home Town Tv Series  (2016), Home Town Takeover TV Series(2021), and Home Town Kickstart TV Series (2022).

As of 2022, she is presently casting in Home Town Kickstart(TV Series).

Erin Napier: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, and Education

Erin Napier was born on August 30, 1985, in Mississippi, USA. Her full name is Erin Raspberry Napier. Erin is the daughter of Phil Raspberry and Karen Raspberry.

By profession, her father is a physical therapist while her mother is a real estate agent.

She has an older brother named Clark Raspberry. She holds American nationality with Caucasian ethnicity.

Education

Erin studied at a local high school. Later, she joined the University of Mississippi. She earns a bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design. She is 36 years old.
