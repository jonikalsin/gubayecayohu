Katherine Webb is pregnant, expecting 3rd son with AJ McCarron

Katherine Webb and AJ McCarron have a new teammate on the way.

On Monday, the wife of the Houston Texans backup quarterback, AJ McCarron, announced they’re expecting their third child together, another son.

“And God said…BAM! YOU’RE A BOY MOM,” Webb captioned an Instagram video of the gender reveal, cheekily adding, “PSA — payers needed for my sanity.”

Webb, 31, and McCarron, 30, who have been married for six years, are proud parents to sons Tripp, 4, and Cash, 2.

A former beauty queen, Webb was thrust into the spotlight during the 2013 national championship game, when commentator Brent Musburger infamously called her a “beautiful woman.” Earlier this year, Webb revealed why she quit “Hollywood.”

“I quit ‘Hollywood’ shortly after bc I realized I genuinely hated being ‘famous’ and having my personal life out there so much,” Webb posted to her Instagram story in January. “But I did some amazing things and met some amazing people along the way.”

Prior to settling down, Webb appeared on the defunct ABC series “Splash,” in addition to a Carl’s Jr. commercial.

“I gave it all up to marry my forever love and have my boys and I would do it all again in a heartbeat,” she continued.
