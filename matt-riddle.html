Matt Riddle Age, Biography

Matt Riddle was born on January 14, 1986, in Allentown, Pennsylvania, USA. He later relocated to Saratoga Springs, New York. He is of American nationality and of American-White ethnicity. His zodiac sign is Capricorn, and he practices Christianity. As of 2023, he is 37 years old. Riddle’s parents and siblings’ names have not been made public.

Matt went to Saratoga Springs High School and won the New York state wrestling title in 2004. He pinned future UFC champion Jon Jones during his high school career. He then went to East Stroudsburg University, where he played for two years and won numerous championships.
