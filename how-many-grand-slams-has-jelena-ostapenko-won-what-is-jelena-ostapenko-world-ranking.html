How many Grand slams has Jeena Ostapenko won? What is Jeena Ostapenko world ranking?

Jelena Ostapenko is a 25-year-old Latvian professional tennis player. She won the French Open singles title in 2017. Ostapenko is the first Latvian to win a Grand Slam singles tournament and the first unseeded player since 1933 to win the French Open.

On the ITF Women’s Circuit, she has seven singles and eight doubles titles. Her highest career ranking was achieved in March 2018 as world number 5 in women’s tennis.
