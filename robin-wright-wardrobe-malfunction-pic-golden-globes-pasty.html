[PIC] Robin Wrights Wardrobe Malfunction Pasty Slips Out At Golden Globes Hollywood Life

Robin Wright’s Golden Globes Wardrobe Malfunction

Robin was absolutely stunned to win, but we were more stunned to see what was going on under that dress of hers! The flesh-colored pasty poked its way out during Robin’s speech when she turned sideways towards the backstage.

Jennifer Lawrence At The Golden Globes

It was a quick gaffe, but it was very noticeable and obvious that something was very wrong with Robin’s dress.

Not surprisingly, Robin seemed too caught up in the ecstasy of victory to realize that she had suffered a wardrobe malfunction. But we’re sure when she re-watches her acceptance speech she’ll just yell out, “Damn you, pasties!”

Robin Wright: No Malfunction Is Gonna Hold Her Down

Though it’s not like a little pasty action is really going to bring Robin down — she’s had a great weekend.

Not only did the House of Cards actress triumph over a stacked group of nominees at the Golden Globes on Jan. 12 — and look amazing while doing it — but one day earlier it was revealed that she is engaged to be married! After dating for over a year, Robin and actor Ben Foster announced that they are planning to tie the knot, according to People.

So take that, wardrobe malfunction!
