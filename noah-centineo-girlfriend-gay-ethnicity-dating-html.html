Is Noah Centineo Still with Stassie Karanikolaou? A Look into His Dating History

American actor Noah Centineo is a heartthrob. He is best known for his portrayal of Peter Kavinsky in Netflix's To All the Boys film series. The actor also starred in Netflix's popular movies, including The Perfect Date and Sierra Burgess Is a Loser.

With such acting credits to his name, it is no surprise that people are dying to know anything they can about Noah Centineo's girlfriend.

Noah Centineo Allegedly Dating Stassie Karanikolaou

The Perfect Date actor is allegedly dating social media star Anastasia Karanikolaou, popularly known as Stassie Karanikolaou or "@stassiebaby" on her 10.8M strong Instagram.

The two have been linked together since mid-2020, but they have kept their romance low-key on social media. They are spotted together every now and then, often showing PDA.

On October 29, 2020, Centineo and Karanikolaou were spotted holding each other's hands at a Halloween party in West Hollywood.

However speculations of their break up surfaced this past June after Karanikolaou celebrated her birthday in a grand fashion but Noah was nowhere to be seen in the videos and pictures of the eventful night.

Furthermore, the two haven't been making appearances on each other's social media for a while now. This has added further fuel to the fire of speculations.

All that being said, the alleged couple is yet to confirm their relationship publicly.

Noah's Relationship History with Celebrities

Centineo has dated a few celebrities in the past.

During the initial years of his career in 2013, he was in a relationship with Disney star Kelli Berglund. They co-starred in the 2014 film How to Build a Better Boy. Their relationship was short-lived and ended around the summer of 2014.

After Berglund, Centineo was in a two-year relationship with actress Angeline Appel, from 2016 to 2018.

The actor was then linked with model Alexis Ren in March 2019 after they met through mutual friends. As a couple, Noah Centineo and Alexis Ren made their red carpet debut at the UNICEF Masquerade Ball in October 2019.

However, things didn't go well between them either and they separated after dating for over a year in April 2020.

Besides, the talented actor was also rumored of dating singer Camila Cabello, his To All the Boys co-star Lana Condor, and actress Lily Collins.

Noah Centineo's Gay Rumors

When Centineo posted a picture of him kissing a friend Chase Austin on social media in 2017, it confused his fans to the extent that some of them thought he was gay.

The actor tried to set the record straight by saying he was simply kissing a friend to destroy a common belief that intimacy must be reserved for monogamous relationships. 

In September 2017, he revealed he was professed as gay after denying a casual hook up in a tweet. He maintained it was cool to have intimate relations after a connection rather than with random girls.

Noah Centineo's Rise to Fame

The 25-year-old actor began his acting career in the late 2000s when he appeared in the 2009's film The Gold Retrievers. After that, he moved to television and starred in the TV series Austin & Ally in 2011.

Following that, Centineo worked in several movies and TV shows until he got his breakthrough role as Jesus Adams Foster in the American drama series The Fosters in 2015. Not only did the series bring him to public consciousness, but he was also flooded with many big offers from Hollywood.

After The Fosters, he starred in the series T@gged in 2017, and subsequently, he was cast in To All the Boys I've Loved Before, which turned out to a career-defining project.

Centineo starred alongside actress Lana Condor in To All the Boys film series. 

He Embraces His Mixed Ethnicity

Centineo was born on May 9, 1996, in Miami, Florida, to Gregory Vincent Centineo and Kellee Andres.

His father, Gregory, is an  Italian businessman and a film producer, and his mother, Kellee, hails from Germany. 

In October 2018, Centineo disclosed that his ancestry is half Italian, Dutch, and a little bit of Native American during an episode of GQ's Actually Me. He also shared that he had a Puerto Rican connection as well.
