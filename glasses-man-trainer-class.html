Glasses Man (Trainer class) - Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokmon encyclopedia

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

A Glasses Man (Japanese: メガネのおにいさん Glasses Man) is a type of Pokémon Trainer that first debuted in the Generation III games. The Glasses Man appears in Pokémon Colosseum. Like many of Orre's Trainer classes, they do not specialize in any particular strategy or Pokémon.
